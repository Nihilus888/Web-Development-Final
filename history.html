Timeline History
1990 - Singapore’s First Internet Connection
Singapore connects to the global internet for the first time, enabling research and development.
1994 - First Public Internet Service
SingNet launches the first public internet access service, making the internet widely available.
1998 - Broadband Revolution
Singapore launches broadband services, dramatically improving internet speeds and accessibility.
