Purpose of this Page

This page outlines the history and significance of the Internet in Singapore, as well as its impact on society, business, and technology over the years. Singapore has had a relatively short but rapid rise in technology advancements. This website highlights the key events that contributed to the rise of the internet and its impact on the lives of Singaporeans.

Brief History of the Internet in Singapore

Singapore’s journey into the digital age began in the early 1990s, when the country recognized the transformative power of the internet. It started with the precursor called BitNet in 1987 at NUS. With strong government, support and infrastructure investment, Singapore quickly became one of the most connected nations in the world. Today, it boasts some of the fastest internet speeds and highest internet penetration rates globally.
